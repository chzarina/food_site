Our Story

RetroAlino was founded in 2008 with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that combines traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our founder, Chef Ahmad Alino, brought together recipes from his grandmother's kitchen and his formal culinary training to create a unique menu that celebrates both heritage and innovation.

Today, we continue to honor that legacy by serving exceptional food made with the finest ingredients, prepared by skilled chefs who share our passion for culinary excellence.
